Skills To Enhance During This Lockdown




Since the last lockdown, a survey was conducted where it was found that there are two types of people. One who in lethargic mode majorly procrastinate, and the others who use their time wisely. They are those people who have learned the concept of self- growth, who have done something productive like learning a new skill. These people have embraced themselves by learning new ideas about life and careers. Everyone who is reading this must be charged up and eager to know how to be productive, right? And voila, those who want to be skill full and learn new skills, have come to the right place because today you will know about some skills that you should learn during this lockdown.



1) Coding


Well, coding is a high-demand skill in the market. These days we all have seen ads where 6-7 years old children are learning coding. I mean, literally, those who can't even speak alphabets in proper order are now learning this comprehensive computer programming. It is a great skill to comprehend since it improves not only your critical thinking, persistence, and problem-solving ability but also your C.V. 2)App Development

Next, we have app development; if you have already learnt to code, it is just a piece of cake for you guys. If not, it is not a problem because it mainly focuses on design, user experience, and entertainment features. There is an old saying among app developers "Bring your idea to life and get set up for success!". App Development is another high-demand skill in the market. All the companies and aspiring entrepreneurs require this skill are willing to pay $60000 per year.


3) Web Designing

If you like app development skills but thinking it is too complex or tough to digest, then web designing is excellent for you. It is a simpler version of app development since no standard software development kit is required for building a website. I recommend this skill for those who have an artistic side but can't pursue it as a career for whatever reason; you can use it through this skill. In the current market, an average salary of a web developer starts from $42000.


4)Writing

Do you know how a person's work, personality, and learning are judged in society today? Through their writing, how they give words to their thoughts is checked on every level in society. It doesn't matter whether in college, workplace, or community; writing is one of the primary and essential skills for a person to learn. It is required in every field, and those who think what it is in writing to learn? Then let me tell you, writing is widely distributed like SEO copywriting, content writing, copywriting, and many more. A person who writes is great doesn't require a proper degree to earn, as a freelance, part-time, or full-time job, the pay in writing is high.


5) Graphical Designing

Another great skill for the artists breeds to showcase their talent to the world. Graphic Designing does not require any programming. In fact, only software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, In-Design is enough to design your ideas. Almost every company in the world requires graphic designers with a salary ranges from $1500 to $3000 per month.
